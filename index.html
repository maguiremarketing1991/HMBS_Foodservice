Our Story

A family butcher, a single standard, and thirty-five years of refusing to take a shortcut.

01 — FOUNDED 1991

A family business, built by hand

Hugh Maguire opened his first butcher shop on Trimgate Street, Navan, Co. Meath in 1991. In 2004, a second shop followed in Ashbourne. Thirty-five years on, the business remains what it was on day one — family owned, family run, and answerable to no one but the customer at the other end of the counter. Every product that leaves our premises carries the Maguire name, which is why it carries the Maguire standard.

02 — THE CRAFT

Three decades of craftsmanship

Dry ageing is patience applied to beef. Sweet curing and slow smoking are patience applied to pork. It is the same value, applied to every product at every price point: the time is always taken, and no shortcut is permitted. This single discipline — not a recipe, not a machine — is what a foodservice kitchen can rely on, delivery after delivery.

03 — TODAY

Irish quality, built for foodservice

What began as a single counter on Trimgate Street now supplies kitchens across Ireland — hotels, restaurants, cafés, caterers, delis, retailers and distributors — with consistent specification, named Irish provenance, and a supply relationship built to last longer than a single order.
